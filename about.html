About Us

Welcome to Njeiforbi Chicken N Burger, your number one source for freshly cooked chicken and mouth-watering burgers. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our food, with a focus on quality, taste, and customer satisfaction.

Founded in 2018, Njeiforbi Chicken N Burger has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for quality ingredients and unique recipes drove us to start this business. We hope you enjoy our food as much as we enjoy making it for you.

Our Mission

To provide delicious, high-quality chicken and burgers that bring people together and create happy moments.

Our Values

  • Quality: Using fresh ingredients to create tasty meals.
  • Customer Focus: Putting our customers at the heart of everything we do.
  • Community: Supporting local suppliers and engaging with our community.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our menu and service.

Meet Our Team

Our talented chefs and staff are passionate about serving you the best chicken and burgers in town. Every meal is prepared with care and attention to detail.
